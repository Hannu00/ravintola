About En Rico

En Rico is a family-owned pizzeria that has been serving the community since 1990. Our passion for pizza is reflected in our traditional recipes, fresh ingredients, and warm, friendly service.

Our menu features a variety of pizzas, from classic favorites like Margherita and Pepperoni to unique creations like our signature En Rico Special. We also offer a selection of pasta dishes, salads, and desserts.

At En Rico, we believe in the power of pizza to bring people together. We invite you to join us for a meal, share in our passion, and become part of the En Rico family.
