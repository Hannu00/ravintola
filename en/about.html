Pizzeria En Rico

Welcome to Pizzeria En Rico, where the rich tradition of authentic Italian pizza-making has thrived for a century. Established in 1924 by our founder, Antonio Enrico, our pizzeria has been a cherished institution in the heart of our community.

Antonio, a passionate pizza enthusiast from Napoli, brought his family's time-honored recipes and culinary expertise to our bustling city, we continue to honor Antonio's legacy by crafting each pizza with the same dedication to quality and authenticity. Join us as we celebrate 100 years of serving up slices of happiness, one delicious pie at a time. Experience the taste of tradition.
